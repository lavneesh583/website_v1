About IETE

IETE Students' Forum is India's leading recognised, professional and national society devoted to the advancement of Science & Technology. Its Patiala Chapter is looked after by the students of TIET, Patiala. It infuses in the students the true essence of electronics through various technical workshops & events. Mentors provide full guidance & support to the students with live projects. We organize the biggest technical fest of TIET i.e. the ELEMENTOS. Till now it has witnessed its 6 versions!
